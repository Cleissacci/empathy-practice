Inline feedback banner with a built-in variant icon and left accent rule. Keep copy warm and specific.

```jsx
<Alert variant="info" title="Before you begin">
  This module contains guided reflection prompts. Find a quiet space — 15 to 20 minutes is ideal.
</Alert>
<Alert variant="success" title="Reflection saved">Your response has been saved to your private journal.</Alert>
<Alert variant="error" title="Session couldn't be saved">Your draft is preserved locally — try saving again.</Alert>
```

Variants: `info` (teal), `success`, `warning` (amber), `error` (rose). Errors explain the cause and the next step.
